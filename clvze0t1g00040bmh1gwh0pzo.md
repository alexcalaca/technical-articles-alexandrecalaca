## Situation

PC gamers know the ins and outs of the WASD keyboard layout like the back of their handâ€”it's the go-to for navigating virtual worlds. With W, A, S, and D serving as the directional keys on a standard QWERTY keyboard, players enjoy seamless movement while keeping their right hand focused on precise mouse controls.

Yet, every now and then, gamers face a surprising dilemma: their WASD and arrow keys decide to switch roles unexpectedly. Luckily, fixing this layout blip is a breeze.

---

## Solution

Press `FN + W` in your keyboard.
